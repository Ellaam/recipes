Beef Tacos

About This Dish

Taking just a little extra time to make this flavorful ground beef makes all the difference in your homemade tacos!

Ingredients

  • 2 teaspoons dried minced onion
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1 teaspoon chili powder
  • ½ teaspoon cornstarch
  • ½ teaspoon ground cumin
  • ¾ cup purchased peanut sauce
  • ½ teaspoon red pepper flakes (Optional)
  • ¼ teaspoon cayenne pepper (Optional)
  • ¼ cup chopped peanuts
  • ¼ teaspoon dried minced garlic
  • ¼ teaspoon dried oregano
  • 1 pound ground beef
  • ½ cup water

Directions

  1. Mix minced onion, salt, chili powder, cornstarch, cumin, red pepper flakes, cayenne pepper, dried minced garlic, and oregano in a bowl.
  2. Heat a large skillet over medium-high heat. Crumble ground beef into the hot skillet. Cook and stir until the beef is completely browned, 7 to 10 minutes. Drain and discard any excess grease.
  3. Return ground beef to heat. Pour seasoning mixture and water over the beef; stir to combine. Bring to a simmer and cook until the moisture absorbs into the meat, about 5 minutes.
